Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our sweet shop website is open 24/7 for your browsing pleasure! While the stores open at 9 AM to 22.00 PM on Monday-Thursday and 11 AM to 22:00 PM on Friday- Sunday. Whether it's morning, noon, or night, you can always log in to explore our delectable offerings.

What do i get my refund?

Your satisfaction is our priority! In the rare case of any dissatisfaction, we offer a refund policy. Please reach out to our customer service team within 24 hours of your purchase, and we'll swiftly process your refund.

How long it will take food to arrive?

We understand your excitement to enjoy our sweets! Typically, our deliveries take around 30-45 minutes, depending on your location. Rest assured, we strive to get our treats to your doorstep as swiftly as possible.

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

Yes, we cater to all tastes! Our sweet shop proudly offers a diverse menu with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian delights. From sumptuous vegetarian barfis to flavorful non-vegetarian treats, there's something for everyone.

What is cost of food delivery?

Enjoy the convenience of doorstep delivery! Our standard delivery fee is minimal and varies based on your location. You can view the exact cost during checkout before placing your order.

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Our Pro Membership is designed to reward our loyal customers! To be eligible, simply sign up for our newsletter or create an account on our website. As a Pro Member, you'll enjoy exclusive discounts, early access to new products, and special promotions tailored just for you.
